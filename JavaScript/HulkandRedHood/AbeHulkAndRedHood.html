The Hulk is a fictional superhero appearing in publications by Marvel Comics. Created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby, the character first appeared in the debut issue of The Incredible Hulk. The Hulk possesses incredible strength and his alter ego Dr. Bruce Banner is a genius in nuclear physics. Whenever Banner gets angry, he transforms into the Hulk, a green-skinned creature with immense strength and durability.

Red Hood is a vigilante anti-hero who has been a member of and antagonist to the Batman Family. He is the alter-ego of Jason Todd, a former Robin who was brutally murdered by the Joker. Todd was later resurrected and became the Red Hood, taking on a more violent and lethal approach to crime-fighting than Batman. He is known for his mastery of multiple forms of combat and his signature red helmet.
